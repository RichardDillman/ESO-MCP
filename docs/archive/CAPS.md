# ESO Combat Caps & Maximum Values (Update 48)

## Offensive Caps

### Penetration
- Cap: 18,200
- This is the amount needed to reduce enemy armor to zero.
- Any penetration beyond 18,200 is wasted.

### Critical Chance
- Cap: 100 percent
- Rough conversion: 21,973 crit rating is approximately 100 percent crit chance.

### Critical Damage
- Practical cap: 125 percent
- The game applies diminishing returns beyond 125 percent.
- Groups typically target 115 to 125 percent.

### Damage Done
- No hard cap.
- Buffs are grouped into categories; only the strongest effect in each category applies.
- Categories include: Berserk, Slayer, class modifiers, weapon expert, etc.

## Defensive Caps

### Resistances (Armor)
- Cap: 33,000
- 33,000 resistance equals 50 percent damage mitigation.
- Any resistance beyond 33,000 is wasted.

### Damage Mitigation (Global)
- Hard cap: 90 percent
- All mitigation sources combined cannot exceed 90 percent.

### Block Mitigation
- Hard cap: 90 percent
- Block mitigation stacks additively with other sources until the 90 percent global cap.

### Damage Shields
- Hard cap: 50 percent of your maximum health.
- You cannot exceed this value across combined shields.

### Dodge / Evasion
- Theoretical cap: 100 percent
- Practical values: 20 to 25 percent from Major and Minor Evasion.
- Only reduces damage from area attacks, not single-target.

## Resource Caps

### Max Magicka / Stamina / Health
- No caps.
- Linear scaling with attributes and buffs.

### Recovery (Magicka, Stamina, Health)
- No caps.
- Subject only to practical constraints from buffs and uptime.

## Additional Useful Caps

### Critical Resistance
- No hard cap.
- Typical PvP target ranges from 2,500 to 3,300.

### Movement Speed
- Cap: 200 percent of base movement speed.
- You cannot exceed double your normal running speed.

### Roll Dodge Cost Reduction
- Theoretical cap: 100 percent
- Actual minimum cost cannot reach zero due to ability cost floors.

## Summary
- Penetration: 18,200
- Critical Chance: 100 percent
- Critical Damage: Soft cap around 125 percent
- Resistances: 33,000
- Global Mitigation: 90 percent
- Damage Shields: 50 percent of max health
- Movement Speed: 200 percent
- No caps on max stats or recovery
